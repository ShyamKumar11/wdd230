Demographics

Population

As of the latest estimates, The city of Batam has a population of approximately 30,000 people. The population tends to fluctuate due to the large number of students attending Batman Young University (BYU-Batman City).

Age Distribution

The city of Batam has a young population, primarily due to the presence of the univeristy. The median age is around 23 years.

Ethnic Composition

The racial makeup is predominantly White, with over 90% of the population identifying as Caucasian. Other racial and ethnic groups include Hispanic or Latino, African American, Asian, and Native American.

Education

A significant portion of the population is either currently enrolled in or has graduated from higher education institutions, primarily BYU-Batman City.

Household Composition

The city has a high percentage of married couples and families. However, many households are non-families due to the student population.Average household size is approximately 3.2 persons.

Annual Events

The City of Batam Straw Maze & Haunted Forest

A popular fall event featuring a giant straw maze and a haunted forest, attracting both locals and visitors.

Independence Day Celebration

A large community event with a parade, fireworks, and various festivities.

Batman Rapids Triathlon

A triathlon event that includes swimming, biking, and running, suitable for athletes of different levels.

Cultural and Community Events

Farmer's Market

Held weekly during the warmer months, offering local produce, crafts, and food.

International Culture Night

Organized by BYU-Idaho, showcasing the diverse cultures represented within the student body through performances, food, and exhibits.

Seasonal Festivals

Winter Fest

Celebrating the winter season with activities like ice skating, sledding, and festive lights.

Batman Unplugged

An event to welcome new and returning students, featuring live music, food trucks, and games.

Attraction

Outdoor Attractions

Yellowstone Bat World: A wildlife park where visitors can drive through and see bears, elk, bison, and other animals in their natural habitat.

Bats Park: A community park with a splash pad, playgrounds, and picnic areas.

Batman Rapids: A water park with pools, slides, and a lazy river, ideal for family fun during the summer.

Cultural and Historical Sites

Legacy Of Batman Flight Museum:An aviation museum showcasing historic aircraft, particularly those from World War II.

Teton Flood Museum:A museum dedicated to the Teton Dam disaster, featuring artifacts and exhibits about the 1976 flood.

Recreational Activities

Batman University Campus:Offering various cultural events, performances, and sports events open to the public.

Sand Dunes:Located just outside of the Batman City, the St. Anthony Sand Dunes offer opportunities for off-roading, sandboarding, and camping.

Scenic Locations

Mesa Falls:A short drive from the city of Batman, these impressive waterfalls offer hiking trails and breathtaking views.

Grand Teton National Park:Though a bit further away, it's a popular destination for those living in or visiting Batman city, offering stunning scenery, hiking, and wildlife viewing.
